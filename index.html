Mushrooms are awesome!
Mushrooms come in many shapes and sizes and can be seen littering forest floors, or damp bathrooms. They live off decaying matter of sorts and play a vital role in the recycling of dead matter. Much overlooked, mushrooms are way more than just a tasty topping :D
